The musings line is the quietest and probably the most scattered of the bunch, it's filled with stuff I like and things that make me feel.

It leaves the interchange, stops here for a moment above ground, then dives under the bay and surfaces on the far shore. Some thoughts are like that: they go under for a while before they come back up.

i let out my happiness, euphoria, and grief all on this line, whilst linking it with media i consumed, albums i learnt by heart and everything that makes me feel.

if you're lucky you'll spot a version of me yearning for someone or a version of me absolutely wrecking their mental health for someone they know isn't coming back, or maybe just sharing a moment i adore.

welcome to the musings green line!
